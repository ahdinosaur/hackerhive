what is the hacker hive?

the hacker hive is an initiative in the spirit of existing hacker communities to create cooperative living spaces that subsidise living expenses through collective creative output.

Let's live, work, and play together in the first hacker house!

if this idea appeals to you, please participate and get all your friends who you wish to live with to also participate.

there is no chain of command. nothing will get done until someone decides to do it, so start doing!
