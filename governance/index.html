hive proposals

parties involved

all members of the hive

process involved

spectrum of possibilities:

  • consensus only
  • all but n consensus
  • percent agreement
  • supermajority in favor
  • majority in favor
  • largest minority

hive bank

the hive must keep inventory of its existing resources and accept incoming resources.

parties involved

hive bank admins hive members

error checking

all members of the hive can submit proposals to hive bank as a form of bug reporting
